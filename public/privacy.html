Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Take Note Pro is built and run by Joel at Slow Wave Audio. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and what happens with it.

The short version: we collect the minimum needed to make the app work. We don't sell your data to anyone. We don't run ads. We don't track you across other apps or websites.

What we collect

Account information. When you sign up, we store your email address and a unique user ID. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the name and email associated with that account. This is used to identify your account and sync your data across devices.

Session data. Your timecode logs, notes, mic lists, metadata, and session settings are stored in the cloud so they sync between your devices. This data belongs to you.

Documents. If you upload call sheets, scripts, or other documents, these are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.

Payment information. If you subscribe to Pro, payment is handled entirely by Apple (via the App Store) or Stripe (via the website). We never see or store your card details.

Third party services

We use a small number of trusted services to run the app:

  • Firebase (Google) — for user accounts and cloud data storage. Their privacy policy.
  • Stripe — for processing web subscriptions. Their privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — for managing App Store subscriptions. Their privacy policy.

We do not share your data with anyone else.

How we use your data

Your data is used to:

  • Let you log in and sync sessions across your devices
  • Process your subscription if you upgrade to Pro
  • Send you a password reset email if you request one

That's it. No marketing emails, no analytics profiles, no data sold to third parties.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud) and is protected by industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Your sessions are only accessible to your account.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual sessions from within the app at any time. If you want to delete your entire account and all associated data, email us and we'll take care of it within 7 days.

Children

Take Note Pro is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of the page. We won't reduce your privacy protections without letting you know.
